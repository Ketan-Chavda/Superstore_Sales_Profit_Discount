# Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard 📊
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze retail performance, customer loyalty, and profitability.

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project transforms raw Superstore sales data into actionable business insights. It focuses on identifying high-value customers, regional performance, and product profitability.

## 💡 Key Features
- **Executive KPIs:** Real-time tracking of Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity.
- **Top 10 Customer Analysis:** Identifying top revenue generators and their profit margins.
- **Loyalty Metrics:** Tracking 'Order Count' to distinguish between one-time buyers and loyal clients.
- **Advanced DAX:** Custom measures for Profit Margin %, Order Count, and Total Sales.
- **Interactive Slicers:** Filter by Year and Category to see dynamic performance shifts.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights
- **The Profit Paradox:** Identified "Top" customers by sales (e.g., Sean Miller) who actually have negative profit margins, suggesting a need for price or discount optimization.
- **Regional Leaders:** The West region consistently leads in sales volume.
- **Loyalty Trends:** High-sales customers often have lower order counts, indicating potential for subscription-based models.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Power Query (ETL)
